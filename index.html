Food Gallery

Gangnam Hundred Flowers Chicken

In the 20s of last century, Guangzhou's "four restaurants" were regarded as "the highest restaurants in Guangzhou", and the banquets of these four restaurants are the prototype of today's famous Cantonese cuisine.

Whampoa slippery eggs

Huangpu eggs, also known as Huangpu scrambled eggs, Huangpu slippery eggs. It is said that long ago, the area around Huangpu was still a tidal flat. One evening, a boatman suddenly came to the house, and for a while there was no time to buy vegetables, so he had to entertain the guests with the few remaining eggs, and in a hurry, he cooked them in a style that neither resembled scrambled nor fried eggs; Although it is not the same, the guests are greatly appreciated under the taste.

Eight Treasure Lotus Emperor Duck

Eight Treasure Lotus Imperial Duck is rumored to be Empress Dowager Cixi's favorite famous dish, and Panxi Restaurant "Eight Treasure Lotus Imperial Duck" is quite famous. Made with glutinous rice mung beans, pork belly, salted eggs, yellow chestnuts, ham, mushrooms and other ingredients.

Gold medal soy sauce chicken

Not a single chicken can leave Guangdong! The Peninsula's famous soy sauce chicken, with crispy skin and juicy, is fragrant and makes foodies drool! What's more, it smells like chicken!

Crispy char siu

Crispy char siu is a characteristic traditional dish of Guangdong Province, which belongs to the famous dish of Cantonese cuisine. Because the skin is crispy and tender, crisp and refreshing.
